Frequently Asked Questions

What is Taskify.AI?

Taskify.AI is an intelligent productivity platform designed to streamline your workflow through AI-powered task management, meeting summarization, and ethical flagging of discussions. It helps teams stay organized, efficient, and ensures compliance by identifying potential ethical concerns in real-time.

How does Taskify.AI use AI?

Our AI is trained to understand natural language in meetings, automatically identify action items, summarize key discussions, and detect phrases or topics that might indicate ethical breaches. This saves time and ensures important points are never missed.

Is my data secure with Taskify.AI?

Yes, data security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption protocols and secure servers to protect your information. All data processed by our AI models is anonymized and used strictly for improving your experience and service quality, never for third-party purposes without explicit consent.

Can I integrate Taskify.AI with other tools?

Absolutely! Taskify.AI supports seamless integration with popular calendar applications (e.g., Google Calendar, Outlook Calendar), video conferencing platforms (e.g., Zoom, Microsoft Teams), and various project management tools (e.g., Jira, Asana, Trello). This allows for automatic meeting imports, streamlined note capture, and efficient task management across your existing ecosystem.

What is "Ethics Flagging"?

Ethics Flagging is an AI-powered feature that intelligently analyzes meeting discussions for keywords or conversational patterns that may indicate a potential breach of company ethics, compliance rules, or legal guidelines. When a potential flag is raised, the system can alert designated personnel for review, promoting responsible conduct and mitigating risks. This feature helps maintain high ethical standards within your organization.

How do I change my notification preferences?

You can easily customize all your notification preferences to suit your needs. Simply navigate to the 'Settings' page within Taskify.AI and locate the 'Notifications' section. Here, you'll find various toggles and options to control alerts for task assignments, ethics flags, weekly summaries, and even your preferred meeting note delivery format. Remember to save your changes to apply them.
